Think Before You Scan!

How Devices Get Infected?

When you scan a malicious QR code, it may redirect you to a phishing website, download malware, or steal sensitive information from your device.

Potential Consequences

Infected devices can result in data theft, financial loss, or unauthorized access to your accounts.

Stay safe and always verify the source of a QR code before scanning.
